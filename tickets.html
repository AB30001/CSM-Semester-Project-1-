Useful information

All visitors must pre-book or secure a ticket with a specified time slot before arrival. Tickets can only be procured at the entrance to the Community Science Museum in London, depending on availability. Those possessing a membership card or discount coupon are also required to have a ticket. The genuineness of the coupon or membership will be cross-checked with your ticket at the museum's primary entrance. Young ones under the age of 14 are only allowed inside the museum when accompanied by an adult. Independent adolescents aged 14 and over can roam the museum; however, they are not permitted to supervise younger children. If you're interested in exploring The Observatory, the Community Science Museum's additional venue located in London's Riverside Quarters: Kindly book or obtain your ticket here.
